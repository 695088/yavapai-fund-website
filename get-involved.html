Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join YAMF?

YAMF is open to current university students who are interested in finance and alternative investments. We welcome students from all majors and experience levels.

What is the time commitment?

Members typically commit 5-10 hours per week, including meetings, research, and portfolio management activities. We understand academic priorities come first.

Do I need prior investment experience?

No prior experience is required! We provide comprehensive training and mentorship to help you develop your investment skills from the ground up.

What opportunities will I have?

Members gain hands-on experience in portfolio management, access to industry professionals, networking opportunities, and valuable skills for future careers in finance.
